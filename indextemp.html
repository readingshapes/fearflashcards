While I have been fortunate to have had a wide array of professional experiences in my career, it has made telling my story a bit of a challenge.

In pursuing opportunities that seemed challenging and exciting, I hopped across multiple knowledge domains. What, I wondered, could possibly unite coffee, imports, workforce planning, safety, fundraising, teaching, and student outcomes?

This is what I came up with. These skills are both what I have most consistently developed over time, and are the most important to me. What I have lately discovered, is that I am most passionate about applying all of these skills specifically to the domain of data visualization.
